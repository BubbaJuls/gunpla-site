About Gunpla Hobby PH

Building a community of modelers across the Philippines.

Gunpla Hobby PH is your go-to online shop for authentic Bandai Gunpla kits. From quick HG builds to display-worthy PG masterpieces, we stock grades for every skill level and budget.

We are passionate model builders ourselves — we understand what matters: genuine parts, careful packing, fair prices, and fast replies when you have questions. Every kit we ship is sourced to meet Bandai standards.

Whether you are panel-lining your first RX-78-2 or hunting the latest MG release, we are here to help you build, collect, and display with confidence.
